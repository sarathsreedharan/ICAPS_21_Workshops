Workshop Program

    The workshops are intended to provide a forum to discuss research advances on a selected topic in an informal setting and to promote dissemination and collaboration on new Planning and Scheduling challenges. Specific details for the individual workshops will be provided in the near future.

    Accepted Workshops

    • HPlan: Workshop on Hierarchical Planning
    • IntEx: Workshop on Integrating Planning, Acting, and Execution
    • XAIP: Workshop on Explainable AI Planning
    • WIPC: Workshop on the International Planning Competition
    • HSDIP: Workshop on Heuristics and Search for Domain-independent Planning
    • PRL: Workshop on Bridging the Gap Between AI Planning and Reinforcement Learning
    • KEPS: Workshop on Knowledge Engineering for Planning and Scheduling
    • SPARK: Scheduling and Planning Applications woRKshop
    • PlanRob: Workshop on Planning and Robotics
    • FinPlan: Workshop on Planning for Financial Services
